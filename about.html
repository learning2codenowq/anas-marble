Who We Are

MORE THAN
JUST MARBLE

Anas Bldg. Mat. & Marbles Tr. L.L.C. has been a cornerstone of the UAE's construction and interior industry for over 20 years. Based in the heart of Sharjah's Industrial Area, we have grown from a local supplier into one of the region's most trusted names in premium marble and natural stone.

Our journey began with a simple belief — that quality materials and honest service should be accessible to everyone, from the homeowner renovating a single bathroom to the developer building an entire community.

Today, we serve a diverse client base spanning individual villa owners, interior designers, fitout contractors, construction companies and real estate developers across the UAE.
