Materials

OUR STONE RANGE

We stock a wide variety of natural and engineered stone to suit every taste, budget and application.

Marble

Classic and timeless, our marble selection includes Italian Carrara, Emperador, Calacatta and many more varieties in different colours and vein patterns.

Granite

Exceptionally durable and heat resistant, granite is ideal for kitchen countertops, flooring and exterior applications where strength is paramount.

Travertine

A natural limestone with a warm, earthy character. Popular for flooring, wall cladding and outdoor areas where a natural rustic feel is desired.

Engineered Stone

Consistent in colour and pattern, engineered stone offers low maintenance and high durability — a practical choice for busy kitchens and commercial spaces.
